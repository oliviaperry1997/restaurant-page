The Smoking Salmon

The Smoking Salmon is a classic Cascadian Steakhouse and Cannabis Bar. Our menu is made with fresh ingredients, our drinks are brewed locally, and our cannabis is all home-grown - whether it's eating, drinking, or smoking, we're committed to giving you the best experience!
